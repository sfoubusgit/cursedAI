POLICY-content-and-moderation (v1)

Allowed: AI-generated cursed images/videos (uncanny/surreal/unsettling).
Hard bans: explicit sexual violence, minors, extreme gore, doxxing, threats, illegal content, targeted harassment.
AI-only requirement enforced.

Every item has Report button with reasons:
not_ai, gore, sexual_violence, harassment, doxxing, illegal, other.

Admin actions:
- hide (is_hidden=true)
- remove (status='removed' + reason)
Hidden/removed content must disappear immediately.
